## How it works

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wand-magic-sparkles" title="Private coaching suggestions">
    When you send a message that could be improved, Clarity sends you a private suggestion that only you can see. Click **Replace** to update your original message, or **Didn't like it** to dismiss.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bolt" title="Real-time analysis">
    Clarity analyzes messages as you send them in channels where coaching is enabled. No setup beyond picking your channels.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="sliders" title="Four default coaching flags">
    Clarity checks for disrespectful, passive-aggressive, dismissive, and unclear messages out of the box. Pro users can add custom flags.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Privacy-first">
    Messages are analyzed in real time and not stored after analysis. Only you see coaching suggestions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
